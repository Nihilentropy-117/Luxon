Why I Started Luxon

After years of working in home technology, I saw a pattern: homeowners were frustrated with expensive monthly subscriptions, invasive cloud storage, and impersonal corporate service. They wanted smart home security that respected their privacy and their budget.

I founded Luxon to offer something different - professional smart home installation without the corporate overhead, subscription fees, or privacy concerns. Every system I install uses local storage, giving you complete control over your data.

As a Cincinnati native and local technician, I'm here for the long term. When you call, you get me - not a call center halfway across the country. That's the Luxon difference.
