Featured Project: Complete Home Security Transformation

The Challenge

A Hyde Park family needed comprehensive security coverage for their corner lot property with multiple entry points and limited existing infrastructure. They were frustrated with cloud-based systems and monthly subscription fees.

Our Solution

We designed an 8-camera Reolink system with strategic placement covering all entry points, driveway, and backyard. Integrated a smart video doorbell and upgraded their WiFi network for optimal performance. All footage stored locally on a 4TB NVR with 60-day retention.

Results

  • ✓ Complete property coverage with zero blind spots
  • ✓ Crystal-clear 4K footage day and night
  • ✓ No monthly fees - one-time investment
  • ✓ Easy mobile access from anywhere
  • ✓ Complete privacy with local storage
