Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer financing?

We accept all major credit cards. Payment is 50% deposit, 50% upon completion.

Most systems install in 4-8 hours on a Saturday. We'll give you a specific time estimate during consultation.

No. Unlike ADT/Vivint, we never require contracts. You're free to use our services when you need them.
